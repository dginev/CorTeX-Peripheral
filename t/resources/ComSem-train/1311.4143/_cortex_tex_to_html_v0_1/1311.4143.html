We show that three numerical semigroups ⟨5,6,7,8⟩5678\langle 5,6,7,8\rangle, ⟨3,7,8⟩378\langle 3,7,8\rangle and ⟨3,5⟩35\langle 3,5\rangle are of double covering type, i.e., the Weierstrass semigroups of ramification points on double covers of curves. Combining the result with [5] and [2] we can determine the Weierstrass semigroups of the ramification points on double covers of genus two curves.
